Lecture1-chp 01 02

Proprietorships and Partnerships

  • Advantages
    • Ease of formation
    • Subject to few regulations
    • No corporate income taxes
  • Disadvantages
    • Difficult to raise capital
    • Unlimited liability
    • Limited life

Corporation

  • Advantages
    • Unlimited life
    • Easy transfer of ownership
    • Limited liability
    • Ease of raising capital
  • Disadvantages
    • Double taxation
    • Cost of setup and report filing
